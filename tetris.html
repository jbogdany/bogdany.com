tetris — rules

  • Seven shapes fall one at a time. Move them left or right, rotate them, and choose where they land.
  • Filling an entire row clears it — everything above drops down, and you score points. Clearing several rows in one drop scores much more than clearing them separately.
  • Controls: arrow keys or [\u2190]/[\u2192] to move, up/[\u21b7] or z/[\u21b6] to rotate, down/[\u2193 soft] to drop faster, space/[drop] to slam it straight down, c/[hold] to swap the current piece into storage for later.
  • The dim outline near the bottom of the board is a landing preview — it shows exactly where the piece will settle.
  • Every ten lines cleared raises the level, and the pieces fall faster.
  • The game ends when a new piece has nowhere to spawn. Keep the stack low.
