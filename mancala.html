mancala — rules

  • Each side has six pits and one store, to that player's right.
  • On your turn, pick up all the stones from one of your own nonempty pits and sow them one at a time into each following pit, moving counter-clockwise around the board — including your own store, but skipping over your opponent's store entirely.
  • If your very last stone lands in your own store, you get to go again.
  • If your last stone lands in a pit on your own side that was empty, you capture that stone plus everything in the pit directly opposite it, and all of it goes into your store.
  • Once one side has no stones left to play, the game ends immediately, and the other player sweeps whatever remains on their own side into their store.
  • Whoever has the most stones in their store when it's all over wins.
