solitaire — rules

  • Goal: move all 52 cards to the four foundations, built up by suit from ace to king.
  • The seven tableau columns build downward in alternating colors — a red card sits on a black one, or vice versa.
  • Only the exposed top card of a pile, or a valid ordered sequence, can be moved onto another pile.
  • Only a king (or a sequence starting with one) can be placed on an empty tableau column.
  • Draw from the stock to the waste pile; once the stock runs out, it reshuffles from the waste for another pass.
  • Tap a selected card again to send it straight to a matching foundation.
