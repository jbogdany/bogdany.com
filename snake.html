snake — rules

  • Steer with the arrow keys, WASD, the on-screen buttons, or a swipe on the board.
  • Eating a piece of food grows the snake by one segment and adds to your score.
  • The snake speeds up gradually as it grows — the further you get, the faster it moves.
  • Running into a wall or into your own body ends the run.
  • You can't reverse directly into yourself — a turn has to go left or right of your current direction.
  • [pause] freezes the action (also bound to the spacebar) if you need a break.
