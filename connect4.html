connect4 — rules

  • Players take turns dropping a disc into one of the seven columns.
  • Each disc falls to the lowest open space in that column.
  • First to line up four discs in a row — horizontally, vertically, or diagonally — wins.
  • If the board fills up with no four-in-a-row, the game is a draw.
