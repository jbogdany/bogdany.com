sudoku — rules

  • Fill every row, column, and 3×3 box with the digits 1–9, each exactly once.
  • The darker digits are the starting clues — they cannot be changed.
  • Click a cell to select it, then press a digit key or tap a number below the board.
  • Related cells (same row, column, or box) are highlighted when a cell is selected.
  • Conflicts are shown in red as you fill in digits.
  • Use Backspace, Delete, or 0 to erase a cell. Arrow keys navigate between cells.
