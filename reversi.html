reversi — rules

  • Players take turns placing discs on the board. You are amber; the machine is red.
  • A move is only legal if it flanks one or more of the opponent's discs in a straight line — horizontally, vertically, or diagonally.
  • All flanked discs are flipped to your color.
  • If you have no legal move, your turn is skipped. The game ends when neither player can move.
  • The player with the most discs when the board is full (or when no moves remain) wins.
  • Valid squares are shown as faint dots. Click one to place your disc.
